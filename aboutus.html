About us

Sahyadri hub for Innovations and Entrepreneurship Foundation – SHINE Foundations is a Section 8 company setup by Sahyadri College of Engineering & Management to emphasize Innovation and Entrepreneurship. Over the decade Sahyadri has emerged as an innovation hub in the region through its initiatives to drive community based innovations and practices that have given rise to industries and Start-ups in the region, these work hence forth will be accelerated with community in focus through SHINE Foundation.




Vision


To enable, nurture, incubate and invest in individuals and organizations that focus on community centric - inclusive innovation.




Mission


• To empower communities and its stake holders to solve problems by sensitizing to local problems, educating them to develop problem solving skills and empowering with resources to solve the problem.

• To be a catalyst for change by enabling and facilitating the exchange between Local Govt bodies, Industries, Educational Institutions and People.

• To promote and support Women lead enterprises and Innovations.

• To nurture and provide leaders and visionaries to the community to contribute the goal of nation buildin.
